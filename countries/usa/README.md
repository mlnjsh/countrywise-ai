## Strengths
- Home to all major frontier AI labs (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, Meta, xAI)
- NVIDIA dominance in AI hardware (90%+ market share in training GPUs)
- World's deepest VC ecosystem for AI startups ($67B+ in 2024)
- Top research universities (Stanford, MIT, CMU, Berkeley, Princeton)
- Cloud computing infrastructure (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Most AI talent per capita among large nations

## Weaknesses
- Growing regulatory uncertainty (no comprehensive federal AI law)
- Rising costs of AI research (billion-dollar training runs)
- Talent concentration (over-reliance on immigrant researchers)
- China competition in specific applications (surveillance, manufacturing)
- Public trust concerns around AI safety and job displacement

- **CHIPS Act:** $280B for semiconductor manufacturing

- **NSF AI Research Institutes:** 25 institutes funded across the country
