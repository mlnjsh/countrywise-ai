## Why Canada Matters
- **Birthplace of deep learning:** Geoffrey Hinton (Toronto), Yoshua Bengio (Montreal), Richard Sutton (Edmonton)
- 3 of the "Godfathers of AI" are Canadian-based
- CIFAR (Canadian Institute for Advanced Research) funded the neural network research that created modern AI

## Government Strategy
- **Pan-Canadian AI Strategy (2017):** First national AI strategy globally
- **Phase 2 (2024):** CAD $2.4B for compute, talent, commercialization
- **Immigration:** Global Talent Stream — fast-track visas for AI talent
- **Responsible AI:** Voluntary code of conduct, AIDA (Artificial Intelligence and Data Act)

## Research Output
- 4th globally in AI research publications
- ~8% of top-cited AI papers globally
- NeurIPS co-founded by Canadian researchers
- Strong in: reinforcement learning, NLP, generative models
