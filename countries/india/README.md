## Strengths
- World's largest AI talent pipeline (IITs, IISc, IIIT)
- 2nd largest English-speaking workforce globally
- Massive IT services industry (TCS, Infosys, Wipro integrating AI)
- 1.4B population = massive dataset potential for AI training
- Government commitment: IndiaAI Mission, National Quantum Mission
- Cost advantage: AI development at 60-70% lower cost than US
- Strong diaspora in US tech leadership (Google, Microsoft, Adobe, IBM)

## Weaknesses
- Brain drain to US, UK, Canada, Singapore
- Limited indigenous compute infrastructure (GPU shortage)
- Low R&D spending as % of GDP (0.7% vs 3.4% US, 2.4% China)
- AI adoption gap between top companies and SMEs
- Data privacy and AI governance still evolving
- Limited semiconductor manufacturing capability
