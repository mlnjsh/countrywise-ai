## Key Insights

### Who's Rising Fastest?
1. **UAE** (+8 spots in 2 years) — Massive sovereign wealth investment
2. **India** (+3 spots) — IndiaAI Mission, startup boom
3. **France** (+2 spots) — Mistral AI, Hugging Face success
4. **Saudi Arabia** (+5 spots) — Vision 2030 AI push

### Who's Declining?
1. **Russia** (-4 spots) — Sanctions, brain drain
2. **Japan** (-2 spots) — Slow LLM adoption, demographic challenges

### Biggest Gaps to Watch
- **USA vs China compute gap:** Widening due to chip export controls
- **India's talent vs infrastructure gap:** Massive talent but limited GPU access
- **Europe's research vs commercialization gap:** Strong research, fewer unicorns
