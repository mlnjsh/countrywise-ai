## What's Changing (2024-2025 Trends)

1. **Remote AI work** — Enabling talent to stay in home countries while working for US companies
2. **Reverse brain drain (India)** — Top researchers returning for leadership roles (Krutrim, Sarvam AI)
3. **France rising** — Mistral AI and Hugging Face attracting talent back to Paris
4. **UAE poaching** — Tax-free salaries luring talent from Europe and India
5. **Canada's advantage** — Fastest AI immigration pathway (Global Talent Stream)
6. **China's challenge** — Fewer Chinese students going to US, but domestic ecosystem growing
