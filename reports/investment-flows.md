## Trends

### What's Hot (Increasing Investment)
1. **AI Agents** — +200% YoY, biggest growth category
2. **AI Safety** — +150% YoY, driven by government and philanthropic funding
3. **Foundation Models** — +120% YoY, massive training runs
4. **AI Infrastructure** — +80% YoY, data center boom

### What's Cooling
1. **Generative AI image tools** — Market consolidation, fewer new entrants
2. **AI chatbot wrappers** — Commoditized, hard to differentiate
3. **Autonomous vehicles** — Long timelines dampening hype

### Where Smart Money Is Going Next
1. AI chips designed for inference (not just training)
2. Vertical AI applications (legal, healthcare, finance)
3. AI-powered robotics (physical world AI)
4. Sovereign AI (government-funded national models)
