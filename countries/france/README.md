## Why France Matters
- **Mistral AI** ($6B) — Europe's most valuable AI startup, rivaling US labs
- **Hugging Face** ($4.5B) — world's largest open-source AI platform (French-founded)
- Strong mathematical tradition powering AI research (Fields Medal winners)
- Aggressive government push under Macron's AI strategy

## Government Strategy
- **Macron's AI Plan (2018):** €1.5B initial investment
- **France 2030:** €2.2B for AI research and industry
- **INRIA:** National AI research institute
- **EU AI Act:** France active in shaping EU AI regulation
- **Talent:** Tax incentives for AI researchers, research credit (CIR)
